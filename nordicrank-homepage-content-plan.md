# 9. Google SERP Scanner

**H2:** Build links from sites Google already ranks
**How it works (3 steps):**
1. Enter your main keyword.
2. We scrape Google's top 100 and list the pages/domains already ranking for it.
3. We match them against the inventory and show which you can book a guest post on — with prices.
**Why it's strong:** if a site already ranks for your topic, Google has confirmed its relevance and authority there — often a smarter target than a random high-DR domain.
**Mockup:** keyword input → results table (position, ranking domain, In inventory / Hidden·Managed, price, Add to plan).

---

# 10. Semantic Link Matcher

**H2:** Match your target URL to the most relevant publishers
**How it works (3 steps):**
1. Enter your site or a target URL.
2. We scrape and vectorize the content.
3. We match it against the inventory and top-ranking pages and rank by **cosine similarity** — the most topically relevant sites first.
**Why it's strong:** relevance decides how much value a link passes. Two sites can share a DR and have completely different topical fit.
**Output:** ranked matches with relevance %, a suggested article angle, entities and an anchor suggestion.

---

# 11. Data-driven link valuation

**H2:** Prioritize links by expected value, not gut feeling
**Body:** Built on data-driven SEO research and large-scale URL analysis. We weigh signals — source page quality, topical/entity relevance, anchor text & context, traffic & ranking history, site authority, spam risk — into a single link-equity estimate. A competitor's link can be an *input*, but relevance, placement, strength and purpose decide whether it's worth building.
**Visual:** a "Link valuation model" card with weighted signal bars + a single link-equity score.
